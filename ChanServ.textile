h2. ChanServ
h4. *ChanServ* is the services the allows users to register channels. *ChanServ* also protects channels. *ChanServ* it provides services to channel operators.
<p>The following is table of the ChanServ commands.</p>
|.Command| |.What it does | 
|Ban| |BAN allows you to place a ban on a user in a channel.|
|Clear|		|CLEAR will allow you to clear certain things.|
|Flags|		|The access list.|
|flist|		|Lists available channel flags.|
|HalfOp|	|Lists available channel flags.|
|dehalfop| |Lists available channel flags.|
